Fauda



Fauda ("Chaos in Arabic") tells the story of a undercover soliders team at the led of Doron Kabilio (Lior Raz).
The main task of the undercover forces is to damage the Hamas infrastructure in the West Bank operated by Gaza, reaching the top of the organization's military wing in the Gaza Strip.
